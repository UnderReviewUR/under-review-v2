## Summary

- **Server-side quota on `/api/ur-take`**: Free-tier users now hit a durable daily limit (3 UTC) before Anthropic is called — reserve on entry, release on failure, attach `freeQuota` on success.
- **Foundation already on `main`** (`c1a78d4`): Shared `api/_gateQuota.js`, refactored `api/gate.js`, client sync via `freeTierLimits.js` / `App.jsx` / `useTakeAuthHeaders.js`.
- **This branch adds the missing bypass fix**: Direct API calls can no longer skip quota when `GATE_SERVER_QUOTA_ENFORCE=1`.

## What it guards against

Free users bypassing the paywall by clearing localStorage, skipping client `consume`, or calling `/api/ur-take` with a valid bearer after exhausting allowance. Quota is tied to verified email in KV and enforced on the take path.
