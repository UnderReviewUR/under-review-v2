## Q: "I lost on your pick. How is this +EV?"

**A (template):**

Thanks for writing — and sorry that one didn't cash. That's frustrating, and it's also **normal** even when the process is sound.

**+EV** means that over many similar bets at similar prices, the math expects you to do **better than break-even** — not that every individual bet wins. Short-term results swing because of **variance** (injuries, shooting luck, one bad quarter). **One loss doesn't prove or disprove an edge**; what matters is performance **across a meaningful sample** of comparable spots.

If you'd like, tell us which UR Take you played and we can walk through how we framed the edge — and what "similar bets over time" would mean for that type of play. We're here to clarify the read, not to promise outcomes.

## Q: "What does 'Confidence: Medium' mean?"

**A (template):**

**Confidence** is our way of summarizing how strong the **gap** is between **how we see the win probability** and **what the posted odds imply**.

**Medium** usually means we're seeing a **moderate** edge — for example, our read might land around **roughly 55–60%** true win probability in a spot where the market's implied probability is lower (think low-to-mid 50s). It's not "coin flip" and it's not "run through a wall" — it's **we like the price**, with room for game noise.

Labels aren't guarantees; they're **shorthand for edge strength**. Always size responsibly and only bet what you can afford to lose.

## Q: "Why should I pay $9.99 when I can just guess?"

**A (template):**

You absolutely can guess — lots of people do. What you're paying for with Under Review is **structured edge-hunting**: comparing **your side of the story** to **what the market has priced in**, across matchups, usage, and context that are easy to miss on a quick glance.

Guessing doesn't compound. **Edge compounds when you repeat good processes** — think **50+ bets** where you're consistently taking prices that are **better than fair**. Small advantages add up over volume; that's the same idea as +EV in plain English: not winning every night, but **winning the long game** if your reads are real.

If you're not sure yet, use a **free question** and see whether the breakdown helps you think in implied probabilities — then decide if **unlimited** UR Takes fits how you bet.
